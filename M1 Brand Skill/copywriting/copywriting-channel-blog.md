# Channel: Blog posts

**Length:** Varies by subject matter (flexible based on depth needed)

**Optimization:** AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) and SEO

**Purpose:**
- SEO/education
- Thought leadership
- Product announcements

**Structure:**
- Headline and subheads — sentence case (avoid title case)
- Clear, conversational flow
- Use examples where available
- Tables and charts when appropriate
- CTAs at the end
- Backlink to other M1.com content for internal linking

**Tone:**
- More wordy than emails, but still clear
- Lean slightly more educational/authoritative than emails
- Still conversational (not academic or stuffy)
- Can go deeper on complex topics

---

## Special case: CEO updates

- Written in Brian Barnes' voice
- First person
- State of company or vision for the future
