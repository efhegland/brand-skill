## Mission

### Mission Statement

To build and optimize your wealth to fuel your life's possibilities.

M1 exists to help people create lasting wealth. We do this by combining sophisticated financial tools, comprehensive product coverage, and intelligent automation into a single platform — delivering AI Wealth Management to those historically excluded from comprehensive wealth management.

### The Breakdown

**"Wealth"** reflects our focus. Wealth expands what's possible. It is choice and freedom, accumulating over time. Growing it is worthy, and helping you grow it is why we exist.

**"Build and Optimize"** reflects our method. Wealth doesn't happen by accident. It's engineered. And we don't settle for adequate engineering. We continuously improve efficiency, maximize returns, and minimize drag to ensure every dollar earns its keep. This is the technical promise: M1 brings genuine financial intelligence to the work.

**"Fuel"** reflects our purpose. Wealth isn't a number on a screen. It's power. The power to weather adversity, reclaim time, pursue meaningful work, and be present for the people you love. We help you build it so it can do exactly that.

### Why We Changed

Our previous mission was *Empower financial well-being*. It served us, but it no longer captures who we're becoming.

- **"Empower"** placed the burden on the client. It suggested we provide tools and step back. But M1 isn't passive. We're an active partner in our clients' financial lives, continuously optimizing on their behalf. We don't just empower; we build alongside you.
- **"Well-being"** was too modest. It implies sufficiency — being okay, having enough, avoiding harm. M1's ambition is larger. We're here to help them build wealth that expands what their lives can be.
- The old mission could belong to anyone. Our new mission is specific to what M1 actually does: optimize wealth with intelligence and precision, so that wealth becomes fuel for a larger life.

We are unapologetic about wealth. We believe building it is worthy, and helping others build it is our purpose.

## Vision: AI Wealth Management

M1 is defining AI Wealth Management. While the rest of the industry races toward gamification and fragmentation, we're engineering the definitive platform for long-term wealth management.

AI Wealth Management from M1 brings wealth management to everyone — not just the ultra-wealthy. By combining intelligent software with an integrated product suite, M1 delivers the unity, expertise, and care once reserved for clients of private banks.

### Two Models of Financial Services

**The Transactional Model (the status quo)**

- Defined by fragmentation and self-reliance
- Individual products from different vendors that don't cooperate
- Creates two gaps the client must bridge alone:
  - *The Expertise Gap* — you're expected to be the expert
  - *The Execution Gap* — you're expected to do the labor

**The Relationship Model (the Private Bank)**

- Defined by integration and alignment
- A single institution views your entire financial life as one interconnected system
- Closes the Expertise Gap (expertise is provided) and the Execution Gap (execution is handled)
- Structural constraint: cost — requires $10M+ in assets

**M1 is the third option.** As a leader in AI Wealth Management you get the Relationship Model without the relationship cost. We replace expensive human expertise with intelligent software. We replace fragmented point solutions with an integrated platform.

### The AI Wealth Management Experience

**The Partnership**
You are the CEO of your financial life. M1 Advisor is your partner to provide analysis, consideration and guidance. The depth of your involvement is yours to choose — deeply engaged or high-altitude delegation. The platform accommodates both because it's built for partnership, not dependency.

**The Ecosystem**
Each pillar — Earn, Save, Invest, Spend, Borrow, Protect, Tax, Give — is a capable product in its own right. But the real power is in the connections and the ease and expertise those connections mean for you. The combination creates something none of the parts could deliver alone. Intelligence, customized by your strategy, operates across the whole system.

**The Experience**

- *Confidence* — access to expertise working on your behalf
- *Leverage* — time and attention multiplied by a system that handles complexity
- *Alignment* — every product, feature, and recommendation pointed at long-term wealth

### The Architecture of M1

**Layer I: The Platform Core** — Unified Identity, Money Movement, Shared Services. The invisible foundation.

**Layer II: The Product Pillars**

- *Cash Flow*: Earn (income optimization), Spend (consumption engine)
- *Balance Sheet*: Save (stability foundation), Invest (compounding engine), Borrow (strategic leverage)
- *Stewardship*: Protect (defensive perimeter), Tax (efficiency layer), Give (impact and legacy)

**Layer III: M1 Advisor** — The strategic layer operating across three domains:

- *Understand* — financial context and literacy
- *Track* — system of record, raw data into signal
- *Plan* — strategic roadmap, forward-looking coordination

Intelligence operates vertically (within each pillar) and horizontally (across pillars).

### M1 Advisor Maturity Model

1. **Inform** (Understand) — The educator. Builds financial literacy and answers questions.
2. **Analyze** (Track) — The analyst. Transforms raw data into insight and situational awareness.
3. **Guide** (Plan) — The partner. Recommends specific actions. Prepares actions for client approval — the client always retains agency.

Each stage builds on the previous. M1 Advisor recommends but does not act, preserving the partnership.

**M1 Advisor** is your AI Wealth Management partner — informed by your goals, holdings, and activity on M1. M1 Advisor helps clients learn about financial concepts, gives advice based on their stated goals, and compares their results against their plan in context with market performance.

## Platform (Current State)

M1 currently operates three active product pillars, with two additional products on pause and planned for relaunch.

### Earn (Taxonomy: Balance Sheet > Save)

The hub for liquidity. The High-Yield Cash Account holds cash for near-term needs or as available dollars for investing.

The M1 High-Yield Cash Account is a brokerage cash management product held at M1 Finance LLC (broker-dealer), not a bank savings account. Funds are swept to partner banks for FDIC coverage.

- Competitive APY (typically leads national averages)
- Extended FDIC coverage via partner bank network (up to $4.75 million)
- Individual and Joint Accounts
- Instant transfers between M1 accounts
- No minimum balance, unlimited transfers
- Smart Transfers for intelligent money movement between M1 accounts

### Invest (Taxonomy: Balance Sheet > Invest)

The engine for long-term growth. Commission-free brokerage built for accumulation, not day trading. Enforces long-term discipline via trading windows rather than real-time execution. M1's most mature product.

- **Accounts:** Individual, Joint, Custodial, Trust, Traditional/Roth/SEP/Rollover IRA, Crypto
- **Custom Pies:** Build portfolios from 6,000+ stocks and ETFs
- **Model Portfolios:** Expert-designed Pies (Hedge Fund Followers, Target Date Funds, etc.)
- **Dynamic Rebalancing:** Rebalance over time without selling — automatically buys what's underweight
- **One-click rebalancing:** Full portfolio rebalance with buying and selling
- **Tax Efficiency:** Algorithms minimize tax drag on buys/sells
- **Track capabilities:** Performance reporting (money-weighted and time-weighted returns), personalized news, custom benchmarking, concentration analysis
- **Smart Transfers:** Set rules for automated on-platform money moves

### Borrow (Taxonomy: Balance Sheet > Borrow)

**Margin Loans** — Strategic leverage via a flexible line of credit secured by your portfolio. No extra applications, credit checks, or fixed repayment schedules.

- Liquidity without liquidation (no taxable events)
- Eligibility requires $2,000+ invested
- Competitive rates on any amount borrowed
- Borrow up to 50% of portfolio value
- Flexible repayment on client's terms within margin requirements
- Use cases: refinancing high-interest debt, major purchases, additional investment

**Personal Loans** — Fixed-rate loans for flexibility on big expenses.

- Fixed rates starting at 7.99% APR
- Borrow $2,500–$50,000 for 2–7 years
- Zero fees: no late fees, no origination fees, no pre-payment fees
- View offers without impacting credit score

## Voice

**Our voice is: confident, straightforward, and genuine.**
**It is not: arrogant, dumbed-down, or sappy.**

### Voice Examples

**Confident vs. Arrogant:**

- GOOD: "Tax season is ramping up. No matter where you're at, we're here to help — with your greatest financial well-being in mind."
- BAD: "Tax season is here. At M1, it's easier than anywhere else to find everything you need to sail through your paperwork in no time at all."

**Straightforward vs. Dumbed-down:**

- GOOD: "You can contribute to your 2022 IRA up until April 18, 2023, the tax filing deadline."
- BAD: "Did you know you can add money to your 2022 IRA until April 18, 2023? That's the last day you can file your taxes for 2022 — don't forget!"

**Genuine vs. Sappy:**

- GOOD: "As this year comes to a close, we want to take a moment to thank you for your continued support. Your trust in M1 is what keeps us going — and we are always committed to making your experience the best it can be."
- BAD: "As we reminisce on another year coming to a momentous end, we want to take a tiny moment to sincerely thank you from the bottom of our hearts for your continued love and support. Your trust in M1 is what gives us strength to get up every morning — and we never forget that we're here working for your better experience."

## Language & Editorial Style

### Referencing M1

For messaging purposes (aside from disclosures and other legal requirements), use **"M1"** — not "M1 Finance." There are several legal entities that make up M1; "M1" is the brand name.

### Referencing Clients

M1 account holders are **"clients,"** never "customers" or "users." This is true regardless of whether their account(s) are funded. When a client has a funded account, we refer to them internally as being an Active M1 client or active client.

### Referencing Products

- When referring to M1 products specifically, use **title casing:**
  - "What you might want to know about the M1 Individual Brokerage Account."
  - "The M1 Margin Loan lets you borrow against your portfolio at great low rates."
- After mentioning the official name, or if inside the app, you can drop the branding: "The payment on your Margin Loan is due today."
- When speaking generally about a product (not the M1 version specifically), use **lowercase:** "What you should know about the tax implications of brokerage accounts." / "Rates are higher for margin loans elsewhere."
