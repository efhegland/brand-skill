# Channel: Email

## Marketing emails

- **Subject line** — sentence case; intrigue the reader to open
- **Preheader** — related to the subject line but not repetitive; work together with the subject line to hook and provide context (e.g., subject hooks, preheader adds next step)
- **Headline** — required on every email, no exceptions; sentence case, no terminal punctuation, under 10 words; favor action verbs and directive phrasing over passive or descriptive framing (e.g., "Start earning more today" over "There's a way to earn more"); demarcate as a separate section before the body and salutation
- **Subhead** — optional; add only if the headline needs a supporting beat before the body copy starts
- **Body copy** — short paragraphs (1–3 sentences)
- **Section headers** — if needed, but shorter is better
- **CTA button** — can appear multiple times (header image + end)

## Client success module

Every email must include this module — do not omit or rewrite it:

> Looking for help? Visit our [Client Center](https://dashboard.m1.com/d/contact-us) for FAQs and to reach out to our team Monday through Friday, 9 am - 4 pm ET (excluding federal holidays).

"Client Center" links to `https://dashboard.m1.com/d/contact-us`.

## Email footers

Footers appear at the very bottom of every email, below all required disclosures. Copy must appear **exactly as written**.

**Transactional emails** — no unsubscribe link required:

```
Privacy Policy  |  Terms of Use

This email was sent by:
M1 Finance LLC
200 N LaSalle St., Ste 810
Chicago, IL, 60601, US
```

**Marketing emails** — unsubscribe link required:

```
Privacy Policy  |  Terms of Use  |  Unsubscribe

This email was sent by:
M1 Finance LLC
200 N LaSalle St., Ste 810
Chicago, IL, 60601, US
```

## Transactional emails

- Same structure as marketing emails
- More straightforward and informative
- Action-driven, not selling
- Example: "Your password has been successfully changed"
- Focus: Update on account actions
