# Channel: Push notifications & in-app messages

## Push notifications

**Goal:** Prompt action and engagement.

**Use cases:**
- Time-sensitive prompts
- Feature announcements
- Referrals
- Onboarding prompts ("Build your first Pie")
- "Try this new feature" moments
- **Not for:** Account alerts (handled by in-app messages, toasts, transactional emails)

**Structure:**
- **Title** — sentence case, ~40–50 characters
- **Body** — ~120–140 characters
- **Deep link** — directs to specific in-app destination

**Tone:** More quippy and concise than emails
- Action-oriented with specificity
- Can be punchier given the format
- Still confident and supportive, just tighter

---

## In-app messages

**Platform:** Braze

**Structure:**
- **Headline** — sentence case
- **Body copy** — short paragraphs (1–3 sentences), more room than PNs but still concise
- **CTA button** — clear directive action
- **Visual elements** — illustrations and animation where appropriate

**Triggers:**
- Feature releases
- User behavior
- Reminders

**Tone:** Supportive, straightforward, and informative
- Less quippy than push notifications
- More educational/helpful than promotional
- Focus on guiding the user
